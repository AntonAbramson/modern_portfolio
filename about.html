San Francisco State University

BS in Computer Science and Marketing Minor

In order to continue with my education, I transferred to San Francisco State University to pursue a BS degree in Computer Science. At SFSU, I was able to deduce my concentrated field of interest and focus on the curriculum I was most passionate about. After a handful of algorithm and software development courses, I realized that a topic I found most interesting was front-end development. Due to my involvement with the scootering industry both in front and behind the scenes, I was introduced to a lot of business and marketing practices that influenced me to pursue a minor in Marketing. Pairing the two fields together, I found Data Analysis to be another topic that sparked my interest. After graduating from SFSU in 2020, I hope to find a position in those respective fields that both challenges me and hones in on the skills I have developed throughout my many experiences.

De Anza College

Transfer to University

I attended De Anza College in Cupertino, CA, and took a variety of classes from psychology and sociology, to astronomy and photography, before discovering my passion for computer science. De Anza offered me introductory and intermediate courses in both C++ and Java, and introduced me to topics such as Data Structures, Assembly Language, and UML Diagrams. Studying at De Anza gave me the opportunity to explore a wide range of subjects, acquiring knowledge that is relevant to my everyday life and a new perspective on how to approach any problem I may face.
