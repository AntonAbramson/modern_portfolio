San Francisco State University

BS in Computer Science and Marketing Minor

To continue my education, I transferred to San Francisco State University in the fall of 2017 to pursue a BS degree in Computer Science. At SFSU, I was able to determine my concentrated field of interest and begin to focus on the curriculum I was most passionate about. After a handful of algorithm and software development courses, I developed a strong interest in front-end development. Due to my involvement with the scootering industry both in front and behind the scenes, I was introduced to a several business and marketing practices, inspiring me to pursue a minor in Marketing. in pairing the two fields together, I found Data Analysis to be another topic that sparked my interest. After graduating from SFSU in 2020, I hope to find a position in one of the aforementioned fields that both challenges me and allows me to further hone the skills I have developed by way of my eclectic set of interests

De Anza College

Transfer to University

After finishing high school, I attended De Anza College in Cupertino, CA, and took a variety of classes, from psychology to sociology, astronomy to photography, before ultimately discovering my passion for computer science. De Anza offered me introductory and intermediate courses in both C++ and Java, and introduced me to more complex topics such as Data Structures, Assembly Language, and UML Diagrams. Studying at De Anza gave me the opportunity to explore a wide range of subjects and acquire knowledge relevant to my everyday life, in turn cultivating a new perspective on how to approach any problem I may face.
